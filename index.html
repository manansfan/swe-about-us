SWE CCEW

SWE is an education and reform organization that was established more than six decades ago. It primarily aims to advocate the voices of women engineers and provide them with opportunities to excel in the field of Science, Mathematics, Engineering and Technology (STEM).

SWE is recognized for building leadership skills, providing leadership experience, and creating opportunities for exposure to top industrial and educational leaders, for both collegiate and professional members.

SWE Mission

Empower women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders, expand the image of the engineering and technology professions as a positive force in improving the quality of life, and demonstrate the value of diversity and inclusion.

SWE Core Values

SWE Vision

A world with gender parity and equality in engineering and technology.

Why do we need organizations like SWE?

SWE still exists today because of the infamous gender imbalance in the field of engineering. Societal stigma and dogma have discouraged women from taking up courses in the field of science and pose challenges to women who want to climb up the ladder to have an equitable future.

It is in times like these that collective efforts and effective vocalization can bring a stop to the injustices and artificially reinforced gender norms. Encouragement of female students and promotion of Engineering as a field of study for women is a necessary and fundamental function of SWE.

SWE is a driving force for promoting a diverse workforce and inclusion of all, not limited to engineers. SWE actively works to create a community where the profession welcomes and respects individual, personal, and career choices amongst all cultures.
